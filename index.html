Can Do Canines

Website Redesign

Can Do Canines is a non-profit organization based in Minnesota that is dedicated to providing the perfect assist dog for each of their humans. With such a powerful mission, Can Do Canines deserve a website that is equally as powerful and accessible. My team and I wanted to find a way to make it easier for users to find their perfect forever companion. Through user research, research synthesis, and ideation my team developed a mobile and desktop version of a redesigned Can Do Canines website.

Problem Statement

First-time service dog adopters need a visual layout of their options when searching for a dog so they can understand each dog's unique qualities and feel a sense of connection to their future companion.

User Insights

People looking for a service dog on Can Do Canines need to know dog profiles and visuals so that they are able to select the dog best fitted for them and their family.

UX Hypothesis

We believe providing pictures and current information about available assistance dogs, as well as refreshing the website to a less formal format will allow Can Do Canines users to get a better feel about finding their forever companion.

This is because allowing users to see what their potential assistance dog will look like and giving them all available information will help ease their concerns about getting an assistance dog.
